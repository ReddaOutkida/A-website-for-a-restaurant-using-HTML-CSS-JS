Discover our founder's Story

In Marrakech Jemaa el-Fna, Belkacem ran a beloved food stand, mastering Moroccan cuisine. He journeyed to Atlanta city, where he worked in different jobs for 9 years, collecting and saving every penny so that he could open "Imim", a homage to his roots. With passion and skill, Belkacem's restaurant transported diners to Marrakech, showcasing the essence of Moroccan cuisine. From humble beginnings to a renowned establishment, Belkacem's story epitomized the power of passion and perseverance in sharing cultural heritage through food.
